Available voice commands for EduAdapt Accessibility Reader:

- "Enable dyslexia"
- "Disable dyslexia"
- "Increase font size"
- "Decrease font size"
- "Increase spacing"
- "Decrease spacing"
- "Color blindness off" or "Turn off color blindness"
- "Protanopia"
- "Deuteranopia"
- "Tritanopia"
- "Achromatopsia"

Use these commands by clicking the "Toggle Mode by Voice" button in the extension popup and speaking the command clearly.
